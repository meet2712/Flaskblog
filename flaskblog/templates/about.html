About Us

FlaskBlog is an intuitive blogging platform that has been built using flask framework from scratch. It allows creators to get started into the world of blogging and let the world know of what they have been keen upon. The blog has been curated like a social media platform, allowing the readers to like, share and comment as they please. The project has been developed under the guidance of python Team lead, Mr.Tapan Parmar and Co-founder and director, Mr. Dharmesh Patel at Inexture Solutions LLP by the Student interns of ICT, Ganpat University.
